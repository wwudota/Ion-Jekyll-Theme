We Are The Western Defense

The Western Defense is Western Washington University's official Dota 2 club. Members are invited to join our Discord, Facebook, and Steam to ask questions, organize fun events, and keep our community connected. If you're looking to play with other club members, hop onto our Discord server and see who's down to party up. We're a friendly bunch, but if someone is misbehaving let an officer know.

Meetings & Events

Official club games are played online on Monday nights at 7 PM and are typically organized through Discord.

Club officers will organize one in-person event per quarter but we also encourage club members to organize events as well.
